Easy Iced Brown Sugar Tea Latte

A layered iced tea latte with black tea, milk, and brown sugar.

Serves:1

Time: 7 mins

Equipment

Electric Kettle

Ingredients

1 cup ice 1/2 cup water
1/3 cup milk 1 English breakfast tea sachet
2 tablespoons water 1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions

Directions

  • 1. Boil water.

    Boil water on the stovetop or in an electric kettle (set it to 208Â°F if yours has a temperature setting). You only need 1/4 cup so it shouldn't take long to boil. Use filtered water if possible.

  • 2. Combine tea sachet and hot water in a glass container.

  • 3. Cover container and steep for 5 minutes.

    Discard tea sachet and add cold water.

  • 4. Combine brown sugar and 2 tablespoons hot water.

    Stir until sugar has dissolved.

  • 5. Put brown sugar simple syrup into a cup and add ice.
  • 6. Pour milk.

    Slowly pour milk directly on top of the ice instead of the brown sugar simple syrup.

  • 7. Pour tea.

    Slowly pour cooled down tea directly on top of the ice instead of into the milk. Stir before drinking.
