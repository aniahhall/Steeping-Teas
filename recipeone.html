Earl Grey Iced Tea with Cream Froth

Iced Earl Grey tea with a decadent creamy foam that's super easy to make.

Serves:1

Time: 10 mins

Equipment

Frother

Ingredients

1/2 cup cold water 1/4 cup hot water
1 Earl Grey tea sachet 1/4 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon sugar 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

Directions

  • 1. Boil water.

    Boil water on the stovetop or in an electric kettle (set it to 208Â°F if yours has a temperature setting). You only need 1/4 cup so it shouldn't take long to boil. Use filtered water if possible.

  • 2. Combine tea sachet and hot water in a glass container.

  • 3. Cover container and steep for 5 minutes.
  • 4. Discard tea sachet and add cold water.
  • 5. Lightly whip heavy cream, sugar, and salt.

    In a tall cup, whip together heavy cream, sugar, salt using a handheld milk frother. It takes about a minute to thicken. You want it to be thickened but still pourable.

  • 6. Pour tea into a cup with ice.

    Leave room at the top for the cream froth.

  • 7. Pour cream froth into the cup with tea.

    With the ice and the fluffy cream froth, everything should blend nicely.
